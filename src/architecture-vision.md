# Architecture Vision

## Designing systems that last

I design solutions as part of a broader ecosystem, not as isolated applications.

## Enterprise thinking

- Clear system boundaries
- Data ownership definition
- Integration-first architecture
- Alignment with enterprise architecture

## Public sector and compliance mindset

I integrate architectural decisions with regulatory and operational constraints.

- Auditability and traceability
- Data protection by design
- Compliance-driven architecture
- Risk-aware decision making

## End-to-end ownership

I act across the full lifecycle of solutions:

- Discovery and requirements structuring
- Architecture design and validation
- Implementation guidance
- Evolution and sustainability planning
